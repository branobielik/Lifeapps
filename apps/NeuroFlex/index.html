NeuroFlex

Android cognitive wellness app and future behavioral research platform by LifeApps.

Research Direction

NeuroFlex is being designed as a cognitive wellness product today and a longitudinal behavioral observatory for future research.

The long-term objective is to characterize behavioral trajectories across cognition, wellness, motivation, social connection, motor interaction, and daily routines.
